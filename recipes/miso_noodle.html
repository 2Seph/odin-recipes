Ingredients

  • 200 g jajang noodles
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • ½ tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • ½ tbsp rice vinegar
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 2-3 tbsp hot water

Steps

  1. Cook your favorite noodles in boiling water.
  2. n a bowl add your remaining ingredients and mix together. This should form a thick and saucy consistency.
  3. Once the noodles are done cooking, add them into the bowl and mix with the sauce. Top with green onions, chili crisp, a crispy fried egg.
